A timeline of diplomatic summits with African states

This interactive map provides insight into the deepening diplomatic ties between African countries and their global partners. It depicts the growing number of countries that have engaged in Africa+1* summits over time, from 1883 to today

Explore the data in two ways: by viewing the summits over time, or per country. To see the growing number of summits over time, click on the ”animate” button below. Thereafter, click on a year to see summits that occurred at that specific time. Use the arrows to access earlier and later entries. To see summits per country, click on the map. Information about specific summits will be displayed on the right of the map.
